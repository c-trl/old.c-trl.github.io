About Me

Christian Tirol is a Data Scientist from the Greater New York City area. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Economics with a Minor in Political Science from Rutgers University, and has completed General Assembly's Data Science course.

Christian works with Python to scrape, mine, munge, explore, analyze, and visualize data in order to create sense out of abstraction and order out of chaos. His experience includes work in E-Commerce, and his interests are meta-analysis, predictive analytics, and economic theory. In his free time, Christian enjoys blogging, basketball, music, and RTS.
